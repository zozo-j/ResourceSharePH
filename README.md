## Bayanihan Spirit

ResourceShare PH embodies the Filipino tradition of bayanihan - community cooperation and mutual assistance, especially during times of crisis.

## Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Navigate through tabs to access different features
3. Share resources or request help from your community
4. All data is saved locally in your browser

## Use Cases

### During Typhoons
- Share generators and power banks
- Coordinate community kitchens
- Organize evacuation transportation
- Request emergency supplies

### Post-Disaster Recovery
- Share construction tools and materials
- Coordinate cleanup efforts
- Organize supply distribution
- Facilitate temporary shelter

### Daily Community Support
- Share household items
- Coordinate carpooling
- Organize community meals
- Mutual aid networks

## Technology

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- Local storage for data persistence
- Mobile-responsive design
- Offline capable

## Community Guidelines

- Verify contact information before sharing
- Prioritize critical and urgent requests
- Practice safety when meeting community members
- Report any misuse of the platform
